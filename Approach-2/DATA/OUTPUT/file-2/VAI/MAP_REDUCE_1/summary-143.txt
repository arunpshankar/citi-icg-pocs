 -  Risk class - categories of risk used to calculate sensitivities-based capital requirement and standardized CVA approach capital requirement.
-  Risk factor - underlying variables affecting the value of a market risk covered position or CVA risk covered position.
-  Risk factor classes - for calculating non-default risk capital measure, include interest rate risk, equity risk, foreign exchange risk, commodity risk, and credit risk.
-  Risk-theoretical profit and loss - daily trading desk-level profit and loss on end-of-previous-day market risk covered positions generated by internal risk management models, considering all risk factors.
-  Residential mortgage-backed security (RMBS) - prime RMBS, mid-prime RMBS, or sub-prime RMBS.
-  Securitization position - market risk covered position that is a securitization exposure.
-  Securitization position non-CTP - securitization position other than a correlation trading position.
-  Small market cap - market capitalization of less than $2 billion.
-  Sovereign position - market risk covered position that is a sovereign exposure.
-  Standardized CVA hedge - CVA hedge that is an eligible CVA hedge, not a basic CVA hedge

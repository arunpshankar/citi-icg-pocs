 1. The proposal requires banking organizations to define their trading desk structure and identify trading desks that are used for internal risk transfers and notional trading desks. 


2. Notional trading desks must satisfy all general requirements for trading desks and those applicable for the models-based measure. 


3. The proposal would require a banking organization to have clearly defined trading and hedging strategies for their trading positions that are approved by senior management. 


4. The proposal would maintain certain operational requirements for active management of market risk covered positions, stress testing, control and oversight, and documentation. 


5. The proposal would strengthen a banking organization's prudent valuation practices by incorporating requirements that build on the agencies' overall regulatory framework for market risk management.

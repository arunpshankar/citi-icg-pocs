 * Key points of the regulatory text on capital requirements for market risk and CVA risk:
  * The rule defines the criteria for determining whether a banking organization is subject to the market risk and CVA risk capital requirements.
  * The rule specifies the calculation methodologies for market risk capital requirements and CVA risk capital requirements.
  * The rule provides exclusions and exemptions for certain banking organizations from the market risk and CVA risk capital requirements.
  * The rule grants the agency authority to make certain determinations and take certain actions, including but not limited to requiring a banking organization to hold an amount of capital greater than otherwise required, if necessary or appropriate to address the level of market risk or CVA risk of the banking organization or to ensure safe and sound banking practices.

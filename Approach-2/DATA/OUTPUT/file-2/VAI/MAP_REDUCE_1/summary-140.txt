 1. The definition of eligible CVA hedge is provided, which can be used to mitigate CVA risk. 
2. CVA risk is the risk of loss due to an increase in CVA resulting from the deterioration in the creditworthiness of a counterparty perceived by the market or changes in the exposure of CVA risk covered positions. 
3. CVA risk covered position is defined as a position that is a derivative contract that is not a cleared transaction. 
4. Delta risk is the risk of loss that could result from changes in the value of a position due to small changes in underlying risk factors. 
5. Equity risk is the risk of loss that could arise from changes in underlying equity risk factors.

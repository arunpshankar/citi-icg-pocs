 - The proposal revises the definition of financial collateral, corrects spelling and footnote errors, and removes expired or obsolete provisions. 
- The proposal does not apply to intermediate holding companies of foreign banking organizations.
- The proposal would broaden the scope of application of the supplementary leverage ratio requirement. 
- The proposal aligns the definition of eligible guarantee with the definition of eligible guarantor. 
- The proposal makes conforming changes to other Board rules that reference the capital rule. 
- The proposal requires all banking organizations that file the FR Y-15 to report data for the total exposures systemic indicator. 
- The proposal has been submitted to OMB for review and approval under the Paperwork Reduction Act.

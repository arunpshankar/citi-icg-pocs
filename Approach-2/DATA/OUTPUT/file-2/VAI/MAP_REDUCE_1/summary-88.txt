 Key points of the CVA Risk Management Requirements:

* A banking organization should identify CVA risk covered positions, CVA hedges, and eligible CVA hedges.
* The organization should have a CVA risk management policy approved by senior management.
* The organization should document all material aspects of CVA risk management.
* The organization should have policies and procedures for determining CVA risk capital requirements.
* The organization should have an independent risk control unit responsible for validating CVA models.
* Senior management should oversee the CVA risk control process.
* The organization should have a regular independent audit review of the CVA risk management process.
* Exposure models used in calculating regulatory CVA should meet certain eligibility requirements.

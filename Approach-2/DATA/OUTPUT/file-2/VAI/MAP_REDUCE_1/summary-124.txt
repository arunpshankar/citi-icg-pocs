 - The regulation concerns risk-based capital requirements for unsettled transactions, guarantees and credit derivatives under the substitution approach. 
- It introduces definitions for delivery-versus-payment (DvP) and payment-versus-payment (PvP) transactions, as well as normal settlement period and positive current exposure. 
- The regulation does not apply to cleared transactions subject to daily receipt and payment of variation margin, repo-style transactions, one-way cash payments on OTC derivative contracts, or transactions with a contractual settlement period longer than the normal settlement period.

 - The final rule implements the standardized approach to measuring counterparty credit risk exposures for all banking organizations. 
- Banking organizations will calculate counterparty credit risk exposures using haircuts prescribed by the agencies. 
- Banking organizations with more sophisticated risk management practices may use their own estimates of haircuts with prior approval from the Board. 
- The final rule also makes conforming changes to the capital rule disclosure requirements and revises the phase-in of the current expected credit losses (CECL) accounting standard for certain banking organizations.

 1. The proposal introduces a standardized approach to calculating market risk capital requirements for banking organizations.
2. The standardized approach would apply to all trading desks, except those that meet certain eligibility criteria and are approved by the primary Federal supervisor to use the internal models approach.
3. Trading desks that fail the PLA test or backtesting requirements would be required to use the standardized approach.
4. The proposal also clarifies the treatment of certain market risk covered positions, such as net short risk positions, securitization positions, and defaulted and distressed market risk covered positions.

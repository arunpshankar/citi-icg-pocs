 1. The proposal defines liquid market economies based on objective criteria, including GDP per capita, 
market capitalization, and trading volume.
2. The proposal groups trading positions into risk buckets based on sector and market capitalization for large market capitalization issuers and by emerging market economy or liquid market economy for small market capitalization issuers.
3. The proposal includes three additional risk buckets for equity indices and other equity indices.
4. The proposal defines commodity risk factors into eleven risk buckets based on commodity classes.
5. The proposal requires a banking organization to establish separate risk buckets for each exchange rate between the currency in which a market risk covered position is denominated and the reporting currency.

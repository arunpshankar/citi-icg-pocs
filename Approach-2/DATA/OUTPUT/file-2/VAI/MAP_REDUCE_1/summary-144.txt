 - Trading and hedging strategies must be approved by senior management. 
- Trading desks must have a clearly defined structure and strategy. 
- Trading desks must have a clearly defined risk scope. 
- The organization must have policies and procedures for actively managing market risk covered positions. 
- The organization must stress test the market risk of its market risk covered positions. 
- The organization must have in place internal market risk management systems and processes. 
- The organization must have a risk control unit that is responsible for the design and implementation of the market risk management system. 
- The organization must have an internal audit function that at least annually assesses the effectiveness of the controls supporting the market risk measurement systems.

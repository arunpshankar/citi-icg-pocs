 1. Exposure models used in calculating regulatory CVA must be part of a risk management framework. 
2. Senior management must oversee the risk control process. 
3. An independent risk control unit must validate the models annually. 
4. The models must be tested against benchmarks and reviewed internally. 
5. A [BANKING ORGANIZATION] must define criteria to assess exposure models and their inputs and have a written policy to describe the process to assess the performance of exposure models and remedy unacceptable performance.

 1. The final rule implements the capital requirements of the Basel III international accord. 


2. The final rule revises the definition of total capital and introduces a new definition of
expanded total capital.


3. The final rule revises the risk-based capital ratio framework, including the leverage ratio, to
incorporate the revised definitions of total capital and expanded total capital.


4. The final rule revises the calculation of the tangible capital ratio for state savings associations.


5. The final rule makes conforming changes to the capital conservation buffer and countercyclical capital buffer rules.

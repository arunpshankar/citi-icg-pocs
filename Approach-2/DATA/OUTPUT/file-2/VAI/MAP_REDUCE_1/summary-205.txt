 - The definitions of "advanced approaches" are removed from 12 CFR 238.130, 238.141, 252.12, and 252.42. 


- The definition of "regulatory capital ratio" is revised in 12 CFR 238.130, 238.141, 238.170, 252.12, and 252.42. The revised definition clarifies that the regulatory capital ratio is a capital ratio for which the Board has established minimum requirements for the company by regulation or order, including, as applicable, any regulatory capital ratios calculated under 12 CFR part 217 and the deductions required under 12 CFR 248.12. 


- In 12 CFR 238.141 and 252.12, the definition of "regulatory capital ratio" is further revised to specify that a savings and loan holding company or state member bank must calculate its regulatory capital ratios using either 12 CFR part 217, subpart D, or 12 CFR part 2

 1. Subparts E and F are amended to replace the existing text with new provisions.


2. The amendments include removing and replacing certain terms and phrases, such as "[AGENCY]", "[BANKING ORGANIZATION]", "[REAL ESTATE LENDING GUIDELINES]", and "[APPRAISAL RULE]".


3. The purpose and applicability section is revised to clarify that the subpart applies to certain categories of national banks and Federal savings associations based on their size and complexity.


4. The risk-weighted assets for securitization exposures section is revised to clarify the capital treatment for transfers of small-business obligations with recourse.


5. The CVA risk-based capital requirements are revised to apply to certain categories of national banks and Federal savings associations based on their size and complexity.

 1. The proposal specifies term-based haircuts for investment grade senior securitization exposures and a 30% market price volatility haircut for other securitization exposures.
2. It requires a 20% haircut for main index equities and gold, 30% for other publicly traded equities and convertible bonds, and 30% for other exposure types.
3. For mutual fund shares, the proposal provides an alternative method to calculate the haircut based on the weighted average of haircuts applicable to the instruments held by the fund.
4. The proposal includes minimum haircut floors for certain eligible margin loans and repo-style transactions with unregulated financial institutions to limit excessive leverage outside the banking system.
5. It defines unregulated financial institutions as those not subject to prudential regulation, including hedge funds and private equity firms.

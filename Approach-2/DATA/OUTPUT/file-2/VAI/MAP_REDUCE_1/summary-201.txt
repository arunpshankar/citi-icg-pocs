 - Deductions from regulatory capital:
  - Goodwill, net of associated deferred tax liabilities (DTLs)
  - Gain-on-sale in connection with a securitization exposure
  - Reciprocal cross holdings
  - Investments in the capital of unconsolidated financial institutions
  - Non-significant investments in the capital of unconsolidated financial institutions
  - Non-common stock significant investments in the capital of unconsolidated financial institutions
- Corresponding deduction approach:
  - Methodology used for deductions related to reciprocal cross holdings, investments in the capital of unconsolidated financial institutions, and non-significant investments in the capital of unconsolidated financial institutions
  - Deductions made from the component of capital for which the underlying instrument would qualify if issued by the Board-regulated institution itself
- Non-significant investments in the capital of unconsolidated financial institutions:
  - Deducted if they exceed 10% of the sum of the Board-regulated institution's common equity tier 1 capital elements minus all deductions and adjustments
  - Deductions are net of associated DTLs
  - Board-regulated institution subject to subpart E of this part may exclude the amount of an investment in a covered debt instrument under certain conditions

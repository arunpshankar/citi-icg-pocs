 - A national bank or Federal savings association with $50B or more in total consolidated assets must make public disclosures (except for those subject to subpart E). 
- Subpart E institutions must make public disclosures as described in subpart E. 
- National banks and Federal savings associations must make adjustments for implementing this part as provided in subpart G. 
- A national bank or Federal savings association that changes categories must comply with the requirements of its category no later than the first day of the second quarter following the change. 
- Definitions have been added, revised, or removed.

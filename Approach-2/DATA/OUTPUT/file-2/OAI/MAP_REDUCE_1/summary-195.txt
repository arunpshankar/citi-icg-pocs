For available-for-sale preferred stock classified as an equity security under GAAP, the carrying value of the exposure is calculated by the Board-regulated institution, less any net unrealized gains. For off-balance sheet components of an exposure, the notional amount is multiplied by the appropriate credit conversion factor. 
2. The exposure amount for an OTC derivative contract, a cleared transaction, an eligible margin loan or repo-style transaction, and a securitization exposure is determined under specific sections of the regulation. 
3. A netting set refers to a group of transactions with a single counterparty that are subject to a qualifying master netting agreement and consist only of derivative contracts, repo-style transactions, or eligible margin loans. The net independent collateral amount is the fair value amount of the independent collateral, adjusted by the haircuts, that a counterparty to a netting set has posted to a Board-regulated institution less the fair value amount of the independent collateral posted by the Board-regulated institution to the counterparty.

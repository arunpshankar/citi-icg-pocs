1. The proposed rule includes revisions to current information collections under the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA). The agencies plan to revise and extend for three years the Reporting, Recordkeeping, and Disclosure Requirements associated with Regulatory Capital Rules and Market Risk Capital Rules. 
2. The Board also plans to revise and extend for three years the Financial Statements for Holding Companies, the Capital Assessments and Stress Testing, and the Systemic Risk Report. 
3. Under the Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council (FFIEC), the agencies propose related revisions to the Consolidated Reports of Condition and Income, the Regulatory Capital Reporting for Institutions Subject to the Advanced Capital Adequacy Framework, and the Market Risk Regulatory Report for Institutions Subject to the Market Risk Capital Rule. 
4. The agencies are inviting comments on the necessity of the information collections for the proper performance of the agencies' functions, the accuracy of the agencies' estimates of the burden of the information collections, ways to enhance the quality, utility, and clarity of the information to be collected, ways to minimize the burden of the information collections on respondents, and estimates of capital or start-up costs and costs of operation, maintenance, and purchase of services to provide information.

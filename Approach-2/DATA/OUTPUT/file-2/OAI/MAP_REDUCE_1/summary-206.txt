The definition of "regulatory capital ratio" has been revised to mean a capital ratio for which the Board has established minimum requirements for the company by regulation or order, including any regulatory capital ratios calculated under 12 CFR part 217 and the deductions required under 12 CFR 248.12. The covered company must calculate its regulatory capital ratios using either 12 CFR part 217, subpart D, or 12 CFR part 217, subpart E, whichever subpart resulted in the higher amount of total risk-weighted assets as of the last day of the previous stress test cycle.
2. The definition of "total risk-weighted assets" has been revised to mean the greater of standardized total risk-weighted assets and expanded total risk-weighted assets, each as calculated under part 217 of this chapter.

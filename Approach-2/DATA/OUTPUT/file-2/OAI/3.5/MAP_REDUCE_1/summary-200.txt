(i) Board-regulated institutions subject to specific regulations have a capital conservation buffer that is calculated based on different ratios.
(ii) If a Board-regulated institution's capital ratio is below the minimum requirement, the capital conservation buffer is zero.
(iii) Global systemically important BHCs have a leverage buffer that is calculated based on their supplementary leverage ratio.
(iv) If the supplementary leverage ratio is below 3 percent, the leverage buffer is zero.
(v) The maximum payout ratio for Board-regulated institutions is determined based on their capital buffer requirements.

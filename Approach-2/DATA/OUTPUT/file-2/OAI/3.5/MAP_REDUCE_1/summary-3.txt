1. The Board is proposing revisions to the GSIB surcharge calculation and systemic risk report for large banking organizations.
2. The proposal would remove the use of internal models for credit and operational risk capital requirements, aiming to improve consistency and transparency.
3. Standardized requirements and robust public disclosure would enhance the transparency of capital requirements.
4. The proposal would still require large banking organizations to maintain capital commensurate with their risks and conduct stress tests.
5. The proposal would increase the dollar-based threshold for market risk requirements and revise the calculation method.

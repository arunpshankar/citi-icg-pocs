In section 3.20, all footnotes are to be redesignated as "1" and certain paragraphs are revised to include disclosure requirements for national banks and Federal savings associations subject to subpart E in the event of receivership or liquidation. In section 3.21, certain words are to be removed and replaced to clarify the applicability of minority interest limitations. In section 3.22, various paragraphs are revised to address regulatory capital adjustments and deductions for national banks and Federal savings associations, including the treatment of goodwill, gain-on-sale in securitization exposures, and equity investments in financial subsidiaries. Additionally, there are requirements for deducting net gains and adding net losses related to cash flow hedges and changes in fair value of liabilities.

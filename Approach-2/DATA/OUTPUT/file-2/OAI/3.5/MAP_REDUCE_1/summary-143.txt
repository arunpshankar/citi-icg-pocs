1. Risk class is used to calculate the capital requirement for risk and standardized CVA.
2. Risk factor refers to variables that affect the value of market risk and CVA risk positions.
3. Risk factor classes include interest rate, equity, foreign exchange, commodity, and credit risk.
4. Risk-theoretical profit and loss is the daily profit and loss on market risk positions generated by internal risk management models.
5. Various terms are defined, such as securitization position, small market cap, sovereign position, and trading position.

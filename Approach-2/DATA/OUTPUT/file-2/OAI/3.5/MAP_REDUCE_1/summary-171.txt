The information provided discusses the calculation and measurement of CVA risk for banking organizations. It includes requirements for identifying CVA risk covered positions and eligible CVA hedges, as well as the need for a clearly defined hedging policy. Documentation of CVA risk-based capital requirements and validation of models used for calculating regulatory CVA are also emphasized. The measure for CVA risk can be calculated as the basic measure or the standardized measure, depending on the approval of the agency. The basic measure includes the basic CVA approach capital requirement and any additional capital requirement for CVA risk, while the standardized measure includes the standardized CVA approach capital requirement, the basic CVA approach capital requirement, and any additional capital requirement for CVA risk. The basic CVA approach capital requirement is calculated using specific parameters and formulas.

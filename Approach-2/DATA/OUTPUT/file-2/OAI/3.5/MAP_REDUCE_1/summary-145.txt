1. The banking organization must comply with policies and procedures for market risk and report findings to the board of directors.
2. The banking organization must have a process for valuing market risk covered positions and independent verification of valuation models.
3. The banking organization must assess its overall capital adequacy in relation to market risk, considering risks not captured by standardized measures.
4. The banking organization must demonstrate a comprehensive understanding of securitization positions and regularly evaluate and update the analysis.
5. The banking organization must adequately document all aspects of market risk management, trading desk structure, and internal models.

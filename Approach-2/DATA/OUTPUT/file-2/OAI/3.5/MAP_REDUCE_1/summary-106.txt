The information provided includes various sections and subsections of financial regulations, as well as proposed amendments to market risk information collections and reporting requirements. The proposed changes would require banking organizations to provide public regulatory reports, obtain prior approval for using internal models, and submit confidential supervisory reports. Additionally, there are proposed recordkeeping requirements for credit valuation adjustment and disclosure requirements related to regulatory capital rules. The second part of the information pertains to the proposed revisions of the FR Y-9 family of reporting forms, which are used to collect financial data on holding companies. The revisions aim to align the forms with the proposed changes and would result in an estimated increase in annual burden hours.

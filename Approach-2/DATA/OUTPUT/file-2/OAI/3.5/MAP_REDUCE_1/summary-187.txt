(i) National banks or Federal savings associations that are not subject to subpart E of this part must make their AOCI opt-out election in the Call Report during the first reporting period after they are required to comply with subpart A of this part. If they were previously subject to subpart E, they must make the election in the first reporting period after they are no longer subject to subpart E.
(ii) Subsidiary banking organizations of national banks or Federal savings associations that are not subject to subpart E must elect the same option as their parent institution.
(iii) National banks or Federal savings associations resulting from a merger, acquisition, or purchase transaction and that are not subject to subpart E may change their AOCI opt-out election in their Call Report filed for the first reporting period after they are required to comply with subpart A of this part, with prior notice to the OCC.
(iv) National banks or Federal savings associations must deduct their investments in their own capital or covered debt instruments from their tier 2 capital elements. If they do not have enough tier 2 capital, they must deduct the shortfall amount from the next higher component of regulatory capital.

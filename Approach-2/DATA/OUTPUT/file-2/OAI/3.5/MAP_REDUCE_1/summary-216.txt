The information provided includes amendments to various sections of the regulations related to risk-weighted assets, credit valuation adjustment (CVA) risk, and transition provisions for FDIC-supervised institutions. The amendments involve changes to definitions, capital requirements, and adjustments for certain financial instruments. The amendments also outline the transition periods and percentages for adjustments to be made during the transition period. The revisions aim to ensure compliance with regulatory standards and provide clarity on capital treatment and risk-weighted assets calculations.

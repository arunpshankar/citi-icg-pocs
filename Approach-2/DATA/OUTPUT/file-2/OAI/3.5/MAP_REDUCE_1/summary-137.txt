1. The banking organization must provide qualitative disclosures regarding credit risk mitigation, including policies for netting, collateral valuation, types of collateral, risk concentrations, and credit derivative providers.
2. The banking organization must provide qualitative disclosures regarding securitization, including objectives, risks, roles in the process, monitoring processes, credit risk mitigation policies, and capital approaches.
3. The banking organization must provide qualitative disclosures regarding equity risk, including differentiation of holdings and accounting techniques.
4. The banking organization must provide qualitative disclosures regarding interest rate risk for non-trading activities, including key assumptions and frequency of measurement.
5. The banking organization must provide qualitative disclosures regarding credit quality of assets, including past due exposures, defaulted exposures, and loan modifications.

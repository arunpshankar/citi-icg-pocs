Paragraph (c)(5) states that certain items must be assigned the appropriate risk weight under specific subparts of the regulation. Paragraph (c)(6) allows a national bank or Federal savings association to not deduct a significant investment in the capital of an unconsolidated financial institution if it is made for the purpose of providing financial support, with prior approval from the OCC. Paragraph (30) allows a national bank or Federal savings association to not deduct a significant investment in the capital instrument of a distressed financial institution if it is made for the purpose of providing financial support, with prior approval from the OCC. Paragraph (31) states that items not deducted from common equity tier 1 capital must be included in risk-weighted assets and assigned a 250 percent risk weight. Section 3.34 provides options for calculating exposure amounts for derivative contracts, depending on whether the bank is subject to subpart E or not.

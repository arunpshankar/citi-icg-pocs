 1. The regulation concerns risk-based capital requirements for unsettled transactions, guarantees, and credit derivatives. 
2. Unsettled transactions include delivery-versus-payment (DvP) and payment-versus-payment (PvP) transactions, as well as non-DvP/non-PvP transactions. 
3. Guarantees and credit derivatives are recognized as credit risk mitigation techniques. 
4. Risk-based capital requirements for unsettled transactions depend on the number of business days after the contractual settlement date. 
5. Total risk-weighted assets for unsettled transactions are the sum of the risk-weighted asset amounts of all DvP, PvP, and non-DvP/non-PvP transactions.

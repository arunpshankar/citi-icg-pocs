 1. The proposal generally follows the Basel III calculation steps for non-securitization debt or equity positions.
2. The gross default exposure is calculated as the product of notional amount and LGD, plus cumulative profits (losses).
3. LGD rates are proposed for different categories of instruments, generally consistent with Basel III.
4. Net default exposure allows full or partial offsetting of long and short positions with the same obligor and seniority.
5. Scaling is applied to offsetting treatment for market risk covered positions with maturities of one year or less.

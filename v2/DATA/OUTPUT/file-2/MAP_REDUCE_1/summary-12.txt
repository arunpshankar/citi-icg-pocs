 1.	The proposal defines regulatory residential and commercial real estate exposures and provides criteria for determining whether an exposure is dependent on cash flows from the real estate. 
2.	Regulatory residential real estate exposures are generally assigned lower risk weights than regulatory commercial real estate exposures. 
3.	Exposures that are dependent on the cash flows generated by the real estate are generally assigned higher risk weights than exposures that are not dependent on cash flows. 
4.	The proposal provides examples of exposures that would be considered dependent on cash flows from the real estate and exposures that would not be considered dependent on cash flows. 
5.	The agencies request comment on the application of prudent underwriting standards in the proposed definitions of regulatory residential and regulatory commercial real estate exposures.

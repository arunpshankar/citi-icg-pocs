 1. Delta risk factors and weights are defined for counterparty credit spread risk and reference credit spread risk. 
2. Counterparty credit spread risk is divided into 7 buckets, while reference credit spread risk is divided into 17 buckets. 
3. Delta risk weights for counterparty credit spread risk range from 0.5% to 12%, while delta risk weights for reference credit spread risk range from 0.5% to 12%. 
4. Delta cross-bucket correlation parameters are defined for both counterparty credit spread risk and reference credit spread risk. 
5. The cross-bucket correlation parameters for reference credit spread risk are the same for all credit qualities.

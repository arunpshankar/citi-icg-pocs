 1. The risk weight of an exposure to a sovereign is based on the sovereign's credit risk category (CRC).
2. A sovereign exposure with a CRC of 0-1 is assigned a risk weight of 20%.
3. A sovereign exposure with a CRC of 2 is assigned a risk weight of 50%.
4. A sovereign exposure with a CRC of 3 is assigned a risk weight of 100%.
5. A sovereign exposure with a CRC of 4-7 is assigned a risk weight of 150%.

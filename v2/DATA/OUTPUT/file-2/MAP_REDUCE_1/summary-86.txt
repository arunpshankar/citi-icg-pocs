 1. The proposal would require banking organizations to reflect potential losses from increases in CVA for OTC derivative contracts in risk-weighted assets. 


2. The proposal provides two measures for calculating CVA risk capital requirements: the basic measure for CVA risk and the standardized measure for CVA risk. 


3. The scope of application would apply CVA risk capital requirements to large, complex banking organizations. 


4. The proposal defines a CVA risk covered position as a derivative contract that is not a cleared transaction. 


5. The proposal would allow a banking organization to choose to exclude an eligible credit derivative for which the banking organization recognizes credit risk mitigation benefits from the calculation of CVA risk.

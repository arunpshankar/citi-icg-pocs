 1. A new category, Category IV FDIC-supervised institution, is added. 
2. The definition of "Category II FDIC-supervised institution" is revised to include institutions with $700 billion or more in total consolidated assets and $75 billion or more in cross-jurisdictional activity. 
3. The definition of "Category III FDIC-supervised institution" is revised to include institutions with $250 billion or more in total consolidated assets and at least one of the following: $75 billion or more in total nonbank assets, $75 billion or more in off-balance sheet exposure, or $75 billion or more in weighted short-term wholesale funding.

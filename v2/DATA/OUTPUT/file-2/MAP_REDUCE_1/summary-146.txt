 1. A [BANKING ORGANIZATION]'s capital requirement for market risk is the sum of the non-default risk capital requirement, PLA add-on, standardized approach capital requirement, fallback capital requirement, and capital add-ons. 

2. The non-default risk capital requirement is calculated using the internally modelled capital calculation and stressed expected shortfall. 

3. The fallback capital requirement is the sum of the standardized approach capital requirement for certain market risk covered positions and the absolute value of the fair values of all other market risk covered positions. 

4. A [BANKING ORGANIZATION] may make a re-designation of an exposure or position if it receives prior approval of senior management and documents the re-designation. 

5. For each re-designation, a [BANKING ORGANIZATION] must calculate its capital add-on for re-designation.

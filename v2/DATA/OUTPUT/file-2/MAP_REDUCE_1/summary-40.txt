 1. The proposal defines operational loss, operational loss event, and groups operational loss events into seven event types. 

2. The calculation of average annual total net operational losses would be based on an average of ten years of data. 

3. The proposal includes a $20,000 net loss threshold for inclusion of an operational loss in the calculation of average annual total net operational losses. 

4. A banking organization would group losses with a common underlying trigger into the same operational loss event. 

5. Recoveries would be included in the quarter in which they are paid to the banking organization.

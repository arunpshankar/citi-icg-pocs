 1. The proposal aligns the capital standards for banking organizations subject to Category III or IV capital standards with those subject to Category I or II capital standards. 
2. It requires banking organizations subject to Category III or IV capital standards to recognize most elements of AOCI in regulatory capital. 
3. It applies capital deductions and minority interest treatments that are currently applicable to banking organizations subject to Category I or II capital standards to banking organizations subject to Category III or IV capital standards. 
4. It also applies TLAC holdings deduction treatments to banking organizations subject to Category III or IV capital standards. 
5. The proposal includes a three-year transition period for AOCI.

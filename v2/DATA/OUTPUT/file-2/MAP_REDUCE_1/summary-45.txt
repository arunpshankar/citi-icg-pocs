 1. The proposal revises criteria for determining whether a banking organization is subject to market risk-based capital requirements. 


2. A banking organization with significant trading activity would be required to calculate market risk capital requirements. 


3. The proposed scope is designed to apply market risk capital requirements to all large banking organizations. 


4. The proposal would retain a trading activity threshold and increase the trading activity dollar threshold from $1 billion to $5 billion. 


5. The proposal would help promote consistency and comparability in market risk capital requirements across banking organizations.

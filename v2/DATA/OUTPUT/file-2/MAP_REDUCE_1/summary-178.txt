 1. The regulation describes the parameters for calculating capital requirements for counterparty credit risk, equity risk, commodity risk, and foreign exchange risk. 

2. Counterparty credit risk is divided into 14 credit quality buckets, with different risk weights assigned to each bucket. 

3. Equity risk is divided into 10 delta risk buckets and 2 vega risk buckets, with different risk weights assigned to each bucket. 

4. Commodity risk is divided into 11 delta risk buckets and 1 vega risk bucket, with different risk weights assigned to each bucket. 

5. Foreign exchange risk is divided into separate vega risk buckets for each currency, with a risk weight of 100 percent assigned to each bucket.

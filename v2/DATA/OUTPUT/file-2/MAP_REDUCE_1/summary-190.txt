 1. The final rule amends subparts E and F of part 3 of the OCC's regulations.


2. The final rule revises the risk-weighted asset calculation for securitization exposures and removes the reference to the real estate lending guidelines.


3. The final rule revises the CVA risk-based capital requirements and removes the reference to the appraisal rule.


4. The final rule revises the purpose and applicability of the market risk capital requirements and removes the reference to the regulatory report.


5. The final rule provides for a transition period for Category III and Category IV national banks and Federal savings associations to adjust their capital ratios.

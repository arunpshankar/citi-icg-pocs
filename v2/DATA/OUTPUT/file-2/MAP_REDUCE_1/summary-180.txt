 (1) National banks and Federal savings associations with $50 billion or more in total consolidated assets must make public disclosures described in subpart D. 
(2) National banks and Federal savings associations subject to subpart E must make public disclosures described in subpart E. 
(3) The rule includes transition provisions for national banks and Federal savings associations changing categories. 
(4) The rule revises and adds definitions, including for "Category II national bank or Federal savings association" and "Category III national bank or Federal savings association." 
(5) The rule removes definitions, including for "Advanced approaches national bank or Federal savings association" and "Credit-risk-weighted assets."

 (1) National banks and Federal savings associations with $50B or more in total consolidated assets must make public disclosures described in subpart D. 
(2) National banks and Federal savings associations subject to subpart E must make public disclosures described in subpart E. 
(3) A national bank or Federal savings association that changes categories must comply with the requirements of its category no later than the first day of the second quarter following the change. 
(4) Definitions have been added, revised, or removed to conform with the final rule. 
(5) The effective date of the final rule is 60 days after the date of publication in the Federal Register.

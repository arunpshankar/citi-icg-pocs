 1. The proposal would introduce a capital requirement for CVA risk. 
2. CVA risk is the risk of loss that arises when a counterparty to a derivative transaction defaults. 
3. The proposal would require banking organizations to hold capital against their CVA risk exposures. 
4. The proposal would also allow banking organizations to use certain CVA hedges to reduce their capital requirements. 
5. The proposal would not allow banking organizations to recognize the risk mitigating benefits of CVA hedges that are securitization positions or correlation trading positions.

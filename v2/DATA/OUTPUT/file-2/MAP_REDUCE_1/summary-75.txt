 1. The proposal introduces data quality requirements for modellable risk factors.


2. Risk factors passing the eligibility test but failing to meet any of the seven data quality requirements would be non-modellable.


3. Data should reflect market prices or be reasonably representative of real prices.


4. Data should capture systematic and idiosyncratic risk, volatility, and correlation of risk factors.


5. Data should be updated weekly, and expected shortfall models calibrated to current market prices regularly.

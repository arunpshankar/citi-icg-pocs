 1. The attachment point calculation would include noncash assets in a reserve account and exclude interest rate and foreign exchange derivatives. 


2. The definition of the W parameter would exclude underlying securitization exposures from the numerator for resecuritization exposures. 


3. The delinquency-adjusted (KA) and non-adjusted (KG) weighted-average capital requirement would be modified for SEC-SA. 


4. A supervisory parameter p of 1.0 would be applied to securitization exposures that are not resecuritization exposures and a supervisory parameter p of 1.5 to resecuritization exposures. 


5. A risk weight floor of 15 percent would be applied to securitization exposures that are not resecuritization exposures.

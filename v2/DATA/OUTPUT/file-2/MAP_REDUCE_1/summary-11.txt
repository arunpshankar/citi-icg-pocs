 1. The proposal defines a subordinated debt instrument as any unsecured debt

2. The proposal also includes preferred stock that is not an equity exposure, 
    and any tier 2 instrument or covered debt instrument that is not deducted 
    from regulatory capital, including TLAC debt instruments, and any debt 
    instrument that would otherwise be treated as regulatory capital by the 
    primary federal supervisor of the issuer and that is not deducted from 
    regulatory capital. 
3. The proposal would apply a 150 percent risk weight for exposures that meet 
    the definition of a subordinated debt instrument.

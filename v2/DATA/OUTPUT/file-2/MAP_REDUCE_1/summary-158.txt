 1. Delta sensitivities are assigned to different buckets based on the type of risk, with different risk weights applied to each bucket. 

2. For equity risk, delta sensitivities are further categorized into 13 buckets based on market capitalization and liquidity, with correlation parameters defined for each bucket. 

3. For commodity risk, delta sensitivities are assigned to 11 buckets based on the commodity type, with risk weights and correlation parameters specified for each bucket. 

4. For foreign exchange risk, buckets are established for each exchange rate, with delta sensitivities assigned to each bucket and risk weights applied accordingly. 

5. Cross-bucket correlation parameters are defined for aggregating delta bucket-level risk positions across buckets within each risk class.

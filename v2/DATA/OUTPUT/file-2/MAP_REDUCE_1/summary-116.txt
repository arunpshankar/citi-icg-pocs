 1. The risk weight for a PSE revenue obligation exposure is determined based on the OECD membership and CRC status of the PSE's home country.
2. Real estate exposures are assigned risk weights based on the type of exposure and the LTV ratio.
3. Retail exposures are assigned risk weights based on whether they are regulatory retail exposures or other retail exposures, and whether they have a currency mismatch.
4. Corporate exposures are generally assigned a 100% risk weight, but there are exceptions for certain types of exposures such as investment-grade corporate exposures and project finance operational phase exposures.
5. There is a risk weight multiplier of 1.5 for certain exposures with a currency mismatch.

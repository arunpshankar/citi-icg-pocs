The proposal seeks comment on whether to expand the exclusion for certain debt instruments from fair value option requirements. It also asks for input on the benefits and drawbacks of including ineligible external CVA hedges in market risk capital requirements. The proposal addresses the lack of regulation regarding internal risk transfers within banking organizations, which can misrepresent the risk position. It defines internal risk transfers and establishes requirements for their recognition in risk-based capital requirements. The proposal also outlines the risk-based capital treatment of internal transfers of credit risk, interest rate risk, and CVA risk within banking organizations.

Subparts E and F are being amended. The changes include replacing certain terms and references with new ones. Section 3.100 is being revised to apply to specific types of national banks and Federal savings associations. Section 3.111 is being modified by removing and redesignating certain paragraphs. Section 3.132 is being revised to specify the capital requirements for national banks and Federal savings associations. Sections 3.162 and 3.201 are being amended to update the applicability and requirements for certain institutions. Section 3.300 is being revised to include transition adjustments for certain national banks and Federal savings associations.

The use of collateral and credit risk mitigants can help reduce the credit risk of a banking organization's exposure. These mitigants must be of high quality to effectively reduce credit risk. The proposal would eliminate the use of models for credit risk and replace them with standardized approaches. The proposal also includes requirements for the recognition of credit protection from guarantees and credit derivatives, as well as collateralized transactions. The proposal would define a netting set as a group of single-product transactions subject to a qualifying master netting agreement. The proposal also includes requirements for the recognition of certain debt securities as financial collateral.

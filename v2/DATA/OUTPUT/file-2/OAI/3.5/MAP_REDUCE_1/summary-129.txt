(1) The credit risk mitigant can be financial collateral, an eligible guarantee, or an eligible credit derivative.
(2) The banking organization must transfer credit risk to third parties without certain provisions that could terminate or alter the credit protection.
(3) The banking organization must obtain legal counsel's opinion on the enforceability of the credit risk mitigant.
(4) Clean-up calls in the securitization must be eligible.
(5) Synthetic excess spread is not allowed in synthetic securitization.

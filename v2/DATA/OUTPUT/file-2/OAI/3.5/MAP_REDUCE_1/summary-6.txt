1. The proposed capital standards aim to provide risk sensitivity and transparency based on the size, complexity, and risk profile of banking organizations.
2. The alignment of regulatory capital ratios for large banking organizations would enhance transparency and consistency across these organizations.
3. Banking organizations subject to Category III or IV capital standards would be required to include most elements of AOCI in regulatory capital, similar to Category I or II capital standards.
4. The proposal also includes capital deductions and minority interest treatments applicable to Category I or II capital standards for Category III or IV capital standards.
5. The recognition of AOCI in regulatory capital improves the alignment with market participants' assessment of loss-absorbing capacity and reflects the actual loss-absorbing capacity of banking organizations.

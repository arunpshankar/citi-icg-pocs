The disclosure requirements for market risk capital rules are outlined in 15 tables, with a total reduction of 54 line items. Quantitative disclosures are now included in regulatory reporting. The estimated number of respondents for these requirements is 49, with an estimated annual burden of 127,254 hours. State nonmember banks, state savings associations, and certain subsidiaries have a separate set of requirements, with an estimated number of respondents of 9 and an estimated annual burden of hours 96,530.

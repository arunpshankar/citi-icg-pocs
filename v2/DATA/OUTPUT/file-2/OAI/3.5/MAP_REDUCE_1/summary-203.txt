In this section, a Board-regulated institution can use standard supervisory haircuts or its own estimates of haircuts with prior approval from the Board. There are revisions to various sections, including removing certain words and sentences and revising entries in tables. Transition provisions are introduced, including adjustments for accumulated other comprehensive income (AOCI) and expanded total risk-weighted assets. There are also changes related to the current expected credit losses (CECL) transition. Sections 217.303 and 217.304 are removed and reserved. Lastly, there are amendments to replace the term "an advanced approaches Board-regulated institution" with "a Board-regulated institution subject to subpart E of this part" in multiple sections.

The information provided pertains to the timing and definitions related to FDIC-supervised institutions and their categories. Institutions must comply with the requirements of their category no later than the first day of the second quarter following a change in category. The definitions of various terms are being revised and updated, including the definitions of different categories of FDIC-supervised institutions based on their total consolidated assets and cross-jurisdictional activity. The criteria for each category are outlined, and institutions must meet certain thresholds to be classified in a specific category.

Trading positions include implied volatilities of options for different maturities: 0.5 years, 1 year, 3 years, 5 years, and 10 years. Curvature risk factors for credit spread risk are defined along the relevant underlying credit spread curves. Risk factors for equity risk include delta risk factors (equity spot prices and repo rates), vega risk factors (implied volatilities of equity options), and curvature risk factors (equity spot prices). Risk factors for commodity risk include delta risk factors (commodity spot prices and forward prices), vega risk factors (implied volatilities of commodity options), and curvature risk factors (commodity spot prices or forward prices). Risk factors for foreign exchange risk include delta risk factors (exchange rates), vega risk factors (implied volatilities of options referencing exchange rates), and curvature risk factors (exchange rates).

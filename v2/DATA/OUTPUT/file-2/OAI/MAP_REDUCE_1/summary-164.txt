1. A banking organization must demonstrate that its trading desk consists of similar market risk covered positions to another approved trading desk, or subject the trading desk to the PLA add-on until it provides at least 250 business days of backtesting and PLA test results. 
2. Any changes to the trading desk structure that would result in a material or non-material change in the market risk capital requirement must be approved by the agency or promptly notified to the agency respectively. 
3. The agency can rescind its approval of a model-eligible trading desk or subject it to the PLA add-on if it no longer complies with the requirements. 
4. A banking organization must receive prior approval from the agency to use an internal model for the ES-based measure and must demonstrate that the model properly measures all the material risks, has been properly validated, and meets the applicable requirements. 
5. The banking organization must review its internal models annually, integrate them into its daily risk management process, and validate them initially and on an ongoing basis.

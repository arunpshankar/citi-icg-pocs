In the event of a significant change in an existing instrument, a global systemically important bank holding company (BHC) must update its disclosures at least semiannually. The BHC must also disclose the full terms and conditions of all eligible debt securities. The required information includes details about the issuer, the instrument's legal entity, unique identifier, governing laws, regulatory capital treatment, and more. The BHC must also provide information about the instrument's type, amount recognized in regulatory capital, par value, accounting classification, original date of issuance, and maturity date, among other details. The BHC must also disclose whether the instrument is convertible or non-convertible, and if it is convertible, the conditions under which it will convert.

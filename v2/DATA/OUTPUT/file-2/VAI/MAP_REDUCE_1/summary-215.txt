 1. The final rule implements the risk-based capital rules issued by the Basel Committee on Banking Supervision (Basel III) and endorsed by the Financial Stability Board (FSB). 


2. The final rule revises the risk-weighted asset (RWA) framework for exposures to certain types of counterparties and exposures secured by financial collateral. 


3. The final rule also revises the standardized approach for calculating RWA for derivative contracts and revises the treatment of cleared transactions. 


4. The final rule implements the Basel III leverage ratio framework, which is a non-risk-based measure of capital adequacy. 


5. The final rule revises the risk-based capital framework for exposures to securitizations and revises the definition of “well capitalized” for purposes of the risk-based capital rules.

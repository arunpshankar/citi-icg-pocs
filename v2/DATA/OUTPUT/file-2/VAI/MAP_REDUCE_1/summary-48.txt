 1. The proposal defines market risk covered positions, excluding certain instruments and positions. 
2. Excluded instruments generally are deducted from regulatory capital, explicitly addressed under subpart D or E, have constraints on liquidation and valuation, or are not held with trading intent. 
3. The proposal would make certain changes to the definition, including excluding any derivative instrument or exposure to an investment fund that has material exposures to excluded instruments. 
4. The proposal requests comment on the appropriateness of the proposed definition, challenges in valuing instruments daily, and the exclusion of certain positions. 
5. The proposal also requests comment on the $20 million threshold for identifying significant net short risk exposures and alternatives to the total commitment for loans in calculating notional amount.

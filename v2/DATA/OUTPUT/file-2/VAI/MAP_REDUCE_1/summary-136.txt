 (1) A [BANKING ORGANIZATION] must make specific disclosures about its capital structure, capital adequacy, risk management, and exposures. 

(2) Disclosures must be made publicly available for each of the last twelve quarters. 

(3) The board of directors and senior management are responsible for establishing and maintaining an effective internal control structure over financial reporting. 

(4) One or more senior officers of the [BANKING ORGANIZATION] must attest that the disclosures meet the requirements of this subpart. 

(5) If a [BANKING ORGANIZATION] reasonably concludes that specific commercial or financial information would be exempt from disclosure by the [AGENCY] under the Freedom of Information Act, then the [BANKING ORGANIZATION] is not required to disclose that specific information.

 1. The proposal introduces a capital requirement for operational risk based on a standardized approach. 
2. The current capital rule defines operational risk as the risk of loss resulting from inadequate or failed internal processes, people, and systems, or from external events. 
3. The proposal would require banking organizations to calculate risk-weighted assets for operational risk using the standardized approach for operational risk. 
4. The standardized approach for operational risk would be based on a bank's gross income and would be phased in over a five-year period. 
5. The proposal would also require banking organizations to have a risk management framework in place to manage operational risk.

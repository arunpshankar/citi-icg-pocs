 1. The agencies propose to modify the criteria for including external CVA hedges in the scope of market risk covered positions. 
2. The proposal defines internal risk transfers and establishes requirements for a banking organization to recognize certain types of internal risk transfers in risk-based capital requirements. 
3. The proposal specifies the risk-based capital treatment of internal transfers of credit risk executed from a banking unit to a trading desk to hedge the credit risk arising from exposures in the banking unit. 
4. The proposal specifies the risk-based capital treatment of internal transfers of interest rate risk from a banking unit to the trading desk to hedge the interest rate risk arising from the banking unit. 
5. The proposal specifies the risk-based capital treatment of internal transfers of CVA risk from a CVA desk to a trading desk.
